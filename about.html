About Felt Interiors & Construction

Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform spaces and exceed expectations, Felt Interiors & Construction has been at the forefront of innovative design and quality construction in Kenya. We believe that every space has the potential to be extraordinary, and we're passionate about bringing that vision to life.

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we serve clients nationwide and internationally, delivering exceptional interior design and construction services that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal. Our team of experienced professionals brings together creativity, technical expertise, and unwavering commitment to quality.
